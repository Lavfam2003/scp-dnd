Quick Reference

Action Economy

Each turn you receive: 1 attack action, 1 auxiliary action, 1 bonus action, 1 free action.

Implication: Pistols (bonus) plus a rifle (attack) means a single turn can fire both.

Initiative

Flat contested d20 roll — no modifiers.

Stratagems

The party gains 1 combat point at the start of each round, shared across all members. Spend points to activate class-specific Stratagems. Each class has multiple Stratagem archetypes for different play styles.

Damage Reduction

When you take damage, you may roll a CON check (DC 10 + damage dealt). On success, reduce damage by (character level × 2) + CON bonus in d4s. Uses per long rest equal to your CON modifier.

Sieging

Each side has 10 HP and 10 morale (can fluctuate). HP zero → force retreats. Morale zero → disadvantage on contested checks. Random events affect both. Points of interest provide defensive bonuses; attackers can destroy them.

When you take damage, you may roll a CON check (DC 10 + damage dealt). On success, reduce the damage by (character level × 2) + CON bonus rolled in d4s.

Uses per long rest equal to your CON modifier.

Movement & Ranges
Standard D&D movement. Ranges are measured in inches with a tape measure at the table.
Called Shots
  • Vital Targets (head, heart, etc.) — +4 AC, disadvantage on the attack roll. On hit: auto-crit.
  • Non-Vital Targets (arms, legs) — +2 AC.
    • Arms — target gains disadvantage.
    • Legs — half movement / prone.

Class Features

System Online
Level 1Passive
You gain 2 energy points, distributed between two sub-systems: Strength and Dexterity. Energy use requires you to be wearing a G.O.C Exo Suit.
  • Strength: Increases STR mod equal to energy used.
    • Overdrive: Advantage on STR checks/saves; STR mod +1+energy.
  • Dexterity: Increases DEX mod equal to energy used.
    • Overdrive: Advantage on DEX checks/saves; DEX mod +1+energy.
Calibration Complete
Level 2
Grants a third energy point and a third sub-system: Movement.
  • Movement: +5ft per energy point.
    • Overdrive: This system cannot be overdriven.
System Overdrive
BonusLevel 32/LR
Overdriving a system grants unique buffs at the cost of shutting down that system and its energy for 1d6+1 turns. Overdrives use your bonus action and last 1 turn unless otherwise specified.
Level 5+ features pending
Subclass — Advanced Suit Operator
Suit Energy Repulsion System (E.R.S)
Subclass L1
You gain an additional sub-system: the E.R.S.
  • E.R.S: Reduces incoming damage by double the energy spent.
    • Overdrive (Reaction): Half all incoming damage.
Yellow Suit Class Operator
Subclass L4
You gain access to a G.O.C Yellow Class mech suit — a 10-foot human-operated mech for varied missions. Choose its specific systems and reconfigure them at home base.

In combat, the mech requires your main and auxiliary actions to boot up and enter.
