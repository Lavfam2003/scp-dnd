Frontline battlefield healer. Burst healing, high mobility, keeps people alive under fire. DEX secondary.

"You carry 30 kilograms of medical gear into a firefight and you use every gram of it."
Internal Medic — "Surgeon"
Foundation medical specialist. Mode-select healing (Heal/Cure/Buff), pharmaceutical prep, institutional authority. INT secondary.

"Your clearance level lets you read the unredacted version."

Antimemetics Division cognitive defense operative. Mental protection, mnestic science, BL weapons. INT secondary.

"You didn't stop being a medic — but you became something else too."

What Do I Do On My Turn?

Combat Medic

START → Is someone at 0 HP?
  YES → Can you reach them?
    YES → First Aid (action) + Triage (BA)
    NO → Trauma Extraction (action: grab + drag + patch)
      or Combat Stabilization (reaction: throw canister)
  NO → Is someone hurt?
    YES → First Aid (action) + Triage (BA) on worst off
    NO → Attack (action) or hold

ALWAYS CHECK: Got Triage charges? Use BA to patch. Out of charges? Soft landing (1 last stabilize/condition use).
REACTION: Ally drops? Combat Stab OR Emergency Response (pick one).

Internal Medic

START → Pick your mode (one per action):
  Someone bleeding badly? → HEAL (roll First Aid dice)
  Someone has a condition? → CURE (Purge it, costs charge)
  Ally Frightened/Charmed/Poisoned? → BUFF (Pain Suppression / Steady / Reposition)

BONUS ACTION → Pick one:
  Scan enemy (Clinical Scanner → +1 attack for allies on success)
  Coach ally (Directed Triage → ally spends hit die + your WIS)

REACTION: Enemy attacking ally? Analysis Paralysis (disadvantage).
Ally drops to 0 HP? Crash Cart (burn compound → auto-stabilize at 1 HP).

Memetic Specialist

START → Are there mental threats?
  YES → Ally compromised? Cog Decontam (action: re-save w/ advantage)
    Need area defense? Barrier (action: 15ft adv WIS saves)
    Need to shut one down? Cognitive Strike (action: stun/blind/compel)
  NO → Someone hurt? First Aid (action)
    Nobody hurt? First Aid on self or attack

BONUS ACTION → Pick one:
  Scan enemy (Psych Reading → CHOOSE: Targeting Data (+dmg) or Disruption (disadv on attack))
  Scan ally (Cog Triage → advantage on next save)

REACTION: Ally makes WIS save? Cog Warning (advantage, from L7).

1 Level 1 First Aid, Stabilize, Medical Assessment ▶
First Aid
Action Adjacent
Apply Foundation-grade trauma care: hemostatic gel, dermal adhesive strips, pressure bandages, analgesic autoinjector. Heals 2d6 + WIS modifier HP. Scaling differs by specialization starting at level 5.
Stabilize
Bonus Action Adjacent
Deploy emergency trauma patch on a dying creature. Target stops making death saves and stabilizes at 0 HP. They're not conscious or healed — the patch is buying time.
Medical Assessment
Exploration Examine a Creature
Biometric scanner + hands-on exam. Learn current HP, active conditions, toxins, infections, or anomalous contamination markers. DM may require Medicine check for anomalous effects.
2 Level 2 Emergency Response, Medic's Eye ▶
Emergency Response
Reaction 30ft Range
When an ally within 30 feet takes damage, move up to half your speed toward them without provoking opportunity attacks. Must end closer to the ally. Drilled into every Foundation medic from day one.
Medic's Eye
Bonus Action 30ft Range Medicine Check (enemies)
On an ally: Exact current HP, max HP, and all active conditions. No check required.
On an enemy: Condition tier (healthy/wounded/bloodied/critical) is free. Scanner detail requires a Medicine check — DC set by the DM based on the creature. On failure, the scanner returns noise — you know the tier but can't interpret the detail.
Condition tier is always free so scanning is never wasted. The check gates the useful intel behind enemy difficulty — harder creatures are harder to read.

3 Level 3 — Specialization Entry Triage, Combat Stabilization ▶
Triage
Bonus Action WIS mod / SR Touch
Slap a quick-deploy trauma patch. Heals 1d6 + WIS modifier HP. WIS modifier charges per short rest.

Soft landing: When your last charge is spent, retain one additional use for stabilize (auto, no healing) or condition removal only. Then fully expended.
Side effects: localized numbness ~10 min, mild inflammation. Repeated application → chemical burns.
Combat Stabilization
Reaction 1 / SR 30ft Range
When ally drops to 0 HP, throw a stabilization canister. DEX check — DC 8 (15ft), DC 12 (30ft), DC 16 (40ft at L7+). Success = auto-stabilize. Choose one reaction per trigger — can't use with Emergency Response on same event.

5 Level 5 Adrenaline Shot, Trauma Extraction, FA 3d6 ▶
Adrenaline Shot (Compound 47)
Bonus Action 1 / LR Adjacent, Willing
2d6 temp HP, advantage on next attack, +10ft speed. Duration: target's second turn after injection.
Mental override: If target is Stunned/Dominated/Charmed/Feared, forces immediate re-save (no advantage).
Failure rider: Even on failed re-save, advantage on next natural save against that effect.
Crash: Disadvantage on attacks + ability checks until end of next turn.
Target must be conscious, able to speak, and not under compulsion. Silenced/Dominated creatures cannot consent.
Trauma Extraction
Action NEW v0.12 Adjacent
Grab willing/unconscious creature adjacent to you. Move your full speed dragging them — no opportunity attacks for either of you.
At 0 HP: Auto-stabilize on grab.
End of movement: Free Triage on the extracted creature (costs a charge).
Behind cover: Advantage on death saves until your next turn.
L9 upgrade: Can use as bonus action (no free Triage rider).
Emergency Response gets you in. Trauma Extraction gets you both out.

INTERNAL MEDIC // "SURGEON"

Foundation medical specialist — mode-select healing, pharmaceutical prep, institutional authority
Secondary StatINT
Core MechanicMode-Select: Heal / Cure / Buff
WeaknessLess raw throughput; needs prep time
Reports ToMedical Division (primary)

Mode-Select System

Each First Aid, choose ONE mode:
HEAL — Roll First Aid dice normally. Restore HP.
CURE — Purge a condition. No HP healed. Costs a Purge charge.
BUFF — Directed Recovery compound. No HP healed.

You cannot combine modes. One action, one effect. This is the Internal Medic's core identity.

• IM-7 Immunobooster: Advantage on CON saves vs poison/disease, 8 hrs
• DRG-4 Trauma Plating: 1d6+WIS temp HP until LR
• PS-12 Pain Suppressant: Ignore first Frightened or Stunned (single use)
Precision Medicine (Purge)
Added to First AidWIS mod / SR

4 Level 4 Crash Cart, Directed Triage, Consultation, Scanner ▶
Crash Cart
Reaction30ftNEW v0.13
Trigger: An ally drops to 0 HP within 30ft. Cost: Your reaction + one active Preemptive Treatment compound on that target is consumed. The ally auto-stabilizes at 1 HP instead of falling unconscious.
Creates resource tension — compounds are both ongoing buffs AND emergency saves. Spending a compound to save a life means losing that buff for the rest of the fight.
Directed Triage
Bonus Action2 / SR30ft
Shout medical instructions to a conscious ally. Target spends their hit die + your WIS mod. Target must be able to hear you and chooses whether to accept.
Uses ally's hit dice — drains their short rest healing budget over long days.
Clinical Scanner
Passive + ActiveMedicine Check (enemies)
Passive: Always know ally condition tiers within 30ft (requires consciousness).
Active scan — ally (BA): Exact HP, max HP, all conditions. Automatic, no check.
Active scan — enemy (BA): Condition tier (free) + tactical detail via Medicine check — DC set by DM based on creature difficulty. Clinical-grade scanner gives +2 to this check vs base Medic's Eye.
On successful scan: Allies who can hear you gain +1 to attack rolls vs that target until your next turn.
The +1 attack bonus only triggers on a successful scan. Harder enemies are harder to read, which means the +1 is earned, not automatic.
Classified Consultation
Exploration
Access classified Foundation medical databases via your secure terminal. Medicine check — DM determines what you learn based on your roll. Cross-reference anomalous effects, diseases, or unidentified conditions. Can also research an SCP pre-mission (DM discretion based on clearance).
5 Level 5 Directed Recovery, Analysis Paralysis, Preemptive Treatment +, First Aid 3d6 ▶
Directed Recovery (Buff Mode)
Added to First AidMOVED v0.13
Choose BUFF mode. Pick one compound to administer:
• Pain Suppression (PS-X): Target ignores Frightened, Charmed, and Poisoned effects for 1 turn. Condition is NOT removed — it resumes at the start of target's next turn.
• Steady (NCD-1): Advantage on next saving throw.
• Reposition: Target can use reaction to move half speed.
Lasts until the start of target's next turn.
Pain Suppression is distinct from Purge: Purge (Cure mode) removes conditions permanently. Suppression overrides them for 1 turn only — a combat bandage, not a cure.

Reaction1 / SR30ft

Polypharmacy
PassiveNEW v0.13

Anomalous conditions: Medicine check with advantage.
Physical tracking token recommended — place a marker on allies with active compounds.
Bridges Heal and Cure modes by letting you do both — at the cost of burning a prep compound. Rewards pre-mission planning.

RULES & REFERENCE

Key rules, timing reference, institutional oversight

Key Rules

Anomalous vs Non-Anomalous: If the source has an SCP designation or is clearly supernatural, its effects are anomalous. Mundane humans, animals, conventional traps/tech = non-anomalous. DM has final say on edge cases.

Concentration: One effect at a time. CON save on damage (DC 10 or half damage taken, whichever is higher). Can end voluntarily.

Adjacent: All abilities marked "Adjacent" mean you must be next to the target. No distance tracking needed.

Scene: One continuous situation — from when you arrive or something triggers until the threat resolves, you leave, or the DM moves to the next situation. The DM determines when a scene ends.

Shared Equipment Rule: Allies can use basic trauma patches from a downed medic (no proficiency bonus). Specialization equipment requires training — 50% chance of incorrect use without it.
